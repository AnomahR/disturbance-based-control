\begin{abstract}%
  We present the notions of Markov processes and investigate their relation to semigroups of operators, the so called transition semigroups. We then focus on the special case of Feller processes and their corresponding transition semigroups. We proof a result on the strong continuity of this semigroup and give a canonical construction of a Feller process. We proof Dynkin's formula and investigate the generator of a Feller semigroup. We conclude the paper by presenting a result for the special case of a diffusion processes, hereby linking the concept of Feller processes to partial differential equations.
\end{abstract}%
